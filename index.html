Our Story

Welcome

Voodoo was born out of Rory's obsession with better chicken, his love for the hospitality of New Orleans, and his passion for interacting with people.

Since 2017 we've been serving customers in the heart of London and at festivals around the UK. Join us on our mission to rebuild community by bringing people to a communal place.
